01 WHO WE ARE

The idea was birthed in 2017 and dedicated several years to the design and develop of the digital platform. This concept was brought to life and has since grown into a functional web-based platform, android and iOS app whcih was launched in February 2022. NOIRBLANC Africa Limited is a wholly Zambian owned business that was recently incorporated on 02 July 2020. In January 2022, NOIRBLANC AFRICA LIMITED appointed a board of directors to oversee its strategic vision.
